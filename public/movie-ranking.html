The Best Movies According to Ashish Nandan

From Screen to Soul:My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Shershaah


**Shershaah** is a 2021 Indian biographical war film that pays tribute to the life and legacy of Captain Vikram Batra, a braveheart who made the ultimate sacrifice during the Kargil War of 1999. Directed by Vishnuvardhan and starring Sidharth Malhotra in the lead role, the film captures Batra's unwavering courage, patriotism, and the indomitable spirit that earned him the title "Sher Shah." The narrative seamlessly blends his personal life, including his relationship with Dimple Cheema, played by Kiara Advani, with his heroic battlefield exploits. With its stirring performances, heartfelt music, and gripping storytelling, *Shershaah* stands as a poignant celebration of a national hero who remains an inspiration to millions.


Sam Bahadur


**Sam Bahadur**, the title of the biopic based on Field Marshal Sam Manekshaw, celebrates the extraordinary life and legacy of one of India's most iconic military leaders. Known as "Sam Bahadur" (Sam the Brave), Manekshaw played a pivotal role in shaping India's modern military history. As the Chief of Army Staff during the 1971 Indo-Pak war, his leadership was instrumental in India's decisive victory, which led to the creation of Bangladesh. The film delves into his charismatic personality, unparalleled strategic mind, and unwavering commitment to the nation. Through this cinematic portrayal, viewers gain insight into the life of a leader whose courage and vision continue to inspire generations.


Geetha Govindam


**Geetha Govindam** is a charming Telugu romantic comedy-drama that beautifully blends humor, emotions, and heartfelt storytelling. Directed by Parasuram, the film follows the endearing journey of Vijay Govind (Vijay Deverakonda), a principled and shy young lecturer, who falls for the confident and independent Geetha (Rashmika Mandanna). Their relationship takes a tumultuous turn due to a misunderstanding, leading to moments of hilarity and heartfelt realizations. With its captivating performances, soulful music by Gopi Sundar, and relatable themes of love and trust, **Geetha Govindam** has won the hearts of audiences, becoming a timeless favorite in Indian cinema.
